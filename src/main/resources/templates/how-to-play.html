Welcome to the rules of BulMaze

The rules of BulMaze are very simple, and you don’t need any specific practice or training.

To Start the game - you just click the “PLAY” button.


  The in-game questions are designed to trigger your attention to details along with your critical thinking. Look around carefully! Powerful hints leading to the next clue might be right in front of your eyes. You can play the game on your own, with the help of friends, or you can actually compete with them and see who is faster. Good luck!

STARTING POINT

  • The main entrance of Alexander Nevsky Cathedral Church.

START

  • You will initialize the first question you have to answer.
  • You will be given four possible answers as of course only one of them would be the correct one.
  • The picture on the screen should direct you to a location of a famous site, where you can find the answer.
  • “Show Clue +” button is design to cast a magic spell, and help you with the solution.
  • You can’t continue forward without selecting a possible answer. If you dare to try… “you shall not pass”.
  • By clicking the “Submit Answer” button, your answer would be sent to our team of elite sages.
  • After a blink of an eye you will continue to the next question. The player won’t know if the given answer is correct or not.
  • Once after the game is completed, you won’t be able to go through the questions again. It is one time run.

PAUSE

  • The Player is in their right to pause the game at any moment.
  • Every Player’s progress would be gently stored by the warming cares of our great mother eagles.
  • When the Player is ready to continue with the game, the same just has to click “PLAY” button again, and the game shall continue.

FINISH

  • Once after the successful completion of the game, the correct answers would be revealed.
  • Everybody can see their rank on the global scale of wisdom, by clicking the “Scoreboard” button.
  • As soon as all the question are being answered, the players would have access to the “Quests” section. All the questions would be displayed there.
  • In the “Quests” section could be found enchanting stories, revealing mysterious knowledge.

BulMaze END GAME

  • As part of the rules, each player should share their impressions, thoughts or suggestions. Each review will be evaluated by our council of witchcrafters. Then would be gently displayed among the rest.

BulMaze New Seasons and Updates

  • After passionate discussions on how can we improve the players experience, new updates would be released. Only those who are fearless, brave and ready for new adventures, those who share their emails with BulMaze’s “Newsletter” will be notified when a new release is dispatched.
  • Just go to the “Home” page and scroll to the bottom. You will find the place where you can leave your email, knowing that it is the only way to be informed for every new update.
